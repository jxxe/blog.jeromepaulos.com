SITE_TITLE = the name of the site
EMAIL = the user's login email
PASSWORD = the results of password_hash()
TIME_ZONE = time zone for date_default_timezone_set()
WEATHER_ENDPOINT = example: https://api.weather.gov/gridpoints/MPX/113,70/forecast

GITHUB_TOKEN = personal access token
UPLOADS_REPO = write as username/repo
UPLOADS_BASE_URL = most likely: https://username.github.io/repo/images
